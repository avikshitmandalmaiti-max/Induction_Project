About Collage

Collage is a revolutionary band, formed in 2019 that specialises in bengali rock and blues music. They create a unique blend of traditional and modern sounds, bringing fresh energy to the Bengali music scene.They are one of the few bands in Kolkata to play authentic Bengali Rock. With fresh melodies, they are rapidly becoming one of the most popular bands in Kolkata.

Music

Collage has released several singles and albums that have garnered critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase. Their music is known for its catchy melodies, thought-provoking lyrics, and dynamic arrangements.
